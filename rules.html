Defensive

I wrote a YARA rule to detect different ATM malware families.

Currently present: Alice, DispCash_BR, FiXS and GreenDispenser.

Keep in mind that in some cases, the rule may detect false positives.

Samples and hashes were mainly found here: ATM Malware Wall

And the samples can be downloaded from here by searching for the hash values: VX-Underground
